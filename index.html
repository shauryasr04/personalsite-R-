Developer of ResearchPro

My own efforts with trying to find and contact professors for internships in my area of interest inspired with develop this application. I leveraged my knowledge and exposure to NLP and Python learnt during my externship at parabole I developed this application, which scrapes the web based on relevant search query and then leverages entity recognition capability in spacy to extract the relevant PERSON Entities found. This is current in a proof-of-concept state and i am working to further improve accuracy and performance. Students can instantly find the contact information of professors in a specific college for their field of study.
